Welcome to your Dashboard

This is your Tableau Dashboard. Tableau is an analytics platform, that helps and facilitates the display and transformation of data. We utilize this tool in order to help visualize important information and progress about the users who play our videogame, Rhythm101. The information displayed in this dashboard doesn't contain real life data at this moment, but it is used to show the potential that our game has to offer in terms of analyzing and simplifying all the data that can be recolected by playing our videogame.
